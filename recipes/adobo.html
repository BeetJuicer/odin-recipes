Adobo

Description

This adobo is a really good adobo. I really like this adobo. It is really tasty. It's saucy, savory, just a bit spicy, and best of all, it will Absolutely NOT make you shit your pants. No way, no how.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Green Chili
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Chop the Garlic, Green Chili, and Onions.
  2. Boil the water in a pot.
  3. Throw in the Chicken along with the sliced vegetables.
  4. Pour in the Soy Sauce.
  5. Pour half the amount of Soy Sauce in Vinegar.
  6. Put in sugar.
  7. Let it boil.
  8. Your adobo is finished!
