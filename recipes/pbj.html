Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This PB&J sandwich is a really good sandwich. I really like this sandwich. It is really tasty. It's sweet, soft, NOT spicy, and best of all, it will Absolutely NOT make you shit your pants. No way, no how.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • A jelly of your choice

Steps

  1. Put peanut butter in the first bread.
  2. Put Jelly on the second bread.
  3. Put them together.
  4. Your sandwich is finished!
