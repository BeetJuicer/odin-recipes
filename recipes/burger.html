Burger

Description

This burger is a really good burger. I really like this burger. It is really tasty. It's moist, juicy, NOT spicy, and best of all, it will Absolutely NOT make you shit your pants. No way, no how.

Ingredients

  • Patty
  • Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Cook the patty.
  2. Toast the bun
  3. Mix the mayo and Ketchup
  4. Arrange
    1. Bottom Bun
    2. Lettuce
    3. Patty
    4. Tomato
    5. KetchupMayo
    6. Pickles
    7. Top Bun
  5. Your burger is finished!
